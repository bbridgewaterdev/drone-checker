Features

Everything you need,
before every flight

DroneChecker brings together the data that matters into a single pre-flight check. No faff, no account needed. Core features free — Pro from £2.99/mo.

Intelligent Fly Rating
Green, amber or red — calibrated to your specific drone model. Checks wind speed, gusts, visibility, precipitation, fog and geomagnetic activity simultaneously.
Wind Analysis
Live wind speed, direction and gusts in km/h or mph, with a colour-coded compass and hourly wind forecast so you can see exactly when conditions improve.
Golden Hour CalculatorPRO
Astronomically precise golden hour, blue hour, sunrise and sunset times calculated from your GPS coordinates — no internet required. Includes live countdowns and a "LIVE NOW" indicator for the current window.
KP Geomagnetic Index
Live KP data from NOAA's Space Weather Prediction Center. High geomagnetic activity can cause GPS inaccuracy and compass drift — DroneChecker factors this into your fly rating automatically.
Flight LoggerPRO
Log every flight with date, location, drone, duration, conditions and notes. Export to Excel or Google Sheets for insurance records, compliance documentation or personal reference.
7-Day Best Day PlannerPRO
Can't fly today? See the best day to fly across the next week at a glance. DroneChecker analyses the full forecast and highlights the optimal window for your drone.
Fly Window AlertsPRO
Get notified the moment conditions turn flyable at your saved locations — so you stop manually checking and never miss a weather window.
Fly Rating

One clear answer before you leave the house

DroneChecker analyses seven factors simultaneously — wind speed, gusts, visibility, weather code, precipitation type, fog and KP index — and combines them with your drone's published specifications to give you one simple, honest verdict.

Thresholds are calibrated per drone model. A DJI Mini 4 Pro and a Mavic 3 Pro have different wind limits — DroneChecker knows the difference.
