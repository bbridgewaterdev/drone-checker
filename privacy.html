Privacy Policy

DroneChecker — Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to DroneChecker. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application available at dronechecker.co.uk and on the Google Play Store.

By using DroneChecker, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Who We Are

DroneChecker is an independent application providing drone flying condition information for users in the United Kingdom.

For any privacy-related queries, please contact us at: bbridgewater.dev@gmail.com

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Location Data

DroneChecker requests access to your device's location (GPS) to display local weather, calculate golden hour times, and provide localised flying condition ratings.

We do not store your location data. Location is used only in real time and is never transmitted to our servers or stored anywhere.

3.2 Search Queries

If you use the postcode or town search feature, your query is sent to OpenWeatherMap and Postcodes.io solely to retrieve location coordinates. We do not store any search queries.

3.3 Notification Permissions

If you enable daily fly condition alerts, your preference is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to us.

3.4 No Accounts Required

DroneChecker does not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, or any other personal identifiers.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

DroneChecker may use your browser's local storage to cache app files for offline use via a service worker. This improves performance and allows the app to load with limited connectivity. No personal data is stored in local storage.

6. Children's Privacy

DroneChecker is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at bbridgewater.dev@gmail.com.

7. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold, and to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO). As DroneChecker does not collect or store personal data on our servers, most of these rights are satisfied by the nature of the app itself.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time and will indicate the date of revision at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
