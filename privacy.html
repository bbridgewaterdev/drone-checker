Privacy Policy

DroneChecker — Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to DroneChecker. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application available at dronechecker.co.uk and on the Google Play Store.

By using DroneChecker, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Who We Are

DroneChecker is an independent application providing drone flying condition information for users in the United Kingdom.

For any privacy-related queries, please contact us at: bbridgewater.dev@gmail.com

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Location Data

DroneChecker requests access to your device's location (GPS) to display local weather conditions, calculate golden hour and blue hour times, centre the wind direction map, and provide localised flying condition ratings.

We do not store your location data. Location is used only in real time and is never transmitted to our servers or stored permanently. Your coordinates are passed to third-party weather and mapping services (listed in Section 4) solely to retrieve relevant data.

3.2 Search Queries

If you use the postcode or town search feature, your query is sent to Postcodes.io (for postcodes) or the Open-Meteo Geocoding API (for town and city names) solely to retrieve location coordinates. We do not store any search queries.

3.3 Notification Permissions

If you enable daily fly condition alerts, your notification preference is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to us or any third party. Notifications are scheduled entirely on-device.

3.4 No Accounts Required

DroneChecker does not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, or any other personal identifiers.

3.5 Device Preferences

To improve your experience, the following preferences are saved locally on your device using browser local storage. None of this data is transmitted to us or any third party:

  • Drone profile — your selected drone model, used to personalise wind limit thresholds.
  • Pre-flight checklist state — which checklist items you have ticked, so your progress is preserved between sessions.
  • Cached weather data — the most recently loaded weather conditions, stored with a timestamp to allow the app to display last known data when you have no internet connection at a flying site. This data contains weather readings only and no personal information.

You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser or app cache.

When you use features such as weather, wind map, or location search, your coordinates or search query may be sent to the relevant service. 5. Local Storage and Offline Mode

DroneChecker uses your device's local storage in two ways:

  • Service worker cache — app files (HTML, CSS, scripts) are cached locally so the app loads quickly and works offline.
  • User preferences and weather cache — your drone profile, checklist state, and the most recent weather data are stored locally to improve your experience and enable offline use (see Section 3.5).

No personal data is stored in local storage. All stored data remains on your device and is never transmitted to DroneChecker's servers.

6. Children's Privacy

DroneChecker is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at bbridgewater.dev@gmail.com.

7. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold, and to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO). As DroneChecker does not collect or store personal data on our servers, most of these rights are satisfied by the nature of the app itself. Any locally stored preferences can be cleared by clearing your browser or app cache.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time and will indicate the date of revision at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
