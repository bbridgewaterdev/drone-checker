Privacy Policy

DroneChecker — Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to DroneChecker. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application available at dronechecker.co.uk and on the Google Play Store.

By using DroneChecker, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Who We Are

DroneChecker is an independent application providing drone flying condition information for users in the United Kingdom.

For any privacy-related queries, please contact us at: bbridgewater.dev@gmail.com

DroneChecker Pro subscribers' data is stored and processed using Firebase (Google Cloud), hosted in the EU (europe-west2 region), in compliance with UK GDPR. Free users' data remains on their device only. You may contact the ICO directly at ico.org.uk if you have concerns about how we handle your data.

3. Information We Collect

DroneChecker is designed to collect as little information as possible. The sections below describe what data is used and why.

3.1 Location Data

DroneChecker requests access to your device's location (GPS) to display local weather conditions, calculate golden hour and blue hour times, and provide localised flying condition ratings.

We do not store your location data. Location is used only in real time and is never transmitted to our servers or stored permanently. Your coordinates are passed to third-party weather and map services (listed in Section 4) solely to retrieve relevant data.

3.2 Search Queries

If you use the postcode or town search feature, your query is sent to Postcodes.io (for postcodes) or the Open-Meteo Geocoding API (for town and city names) solely to retrieve location coordinates. We do not store any search queries.

3.3 Analytics Data

DroneChecker uses Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymous usage data. This helps us understand how the app is used so we can improve it. Google Analytics collects:

  • Pages and features visited within the app
  • Session duration and frequency of use
  • Device type, operating system and browser
  • Approximate geographic location (city or region level — not precise GPS coordinates)
  • How you arrived at the app (direct, search, referral)

No personally identifiable information is collected through Google Analytics. Google Analytics 4 anonymises IP addresses by default. Google Analytics sets cookies on your device to distinguish users and sessions (see Section 3.6 for details). Analytics are only enabled after you accept the in-app disclaimer; you can withdraw this consent at any time using the "Reset Data" option in the app footer, which clears all stored data including the consent flag. We do not share this data with any third parties beyond Google's own analytics processing. You can learn more about how Google handles this data at policies.google.com/privacy.

3.4 Accounts and Pro Subscriptions

DroneChecker has a free tier that requires no account. Free users: no personal data is collected or stored on our servers.

DroneChecker Pro is an optional paid subscription (£2.99/month or £29.99/year). If you sign up for Pro, the following personal data is collected and stored:

  • Email address — collected via Google Sign-In or email/password registration, used to identify your account and send payment receipts via Stripe.
  • Google account name and profile photo — if you sign in with Google, your display name and photo URL are stored to personalise your experience.
  • Flight log entries — if you use the flight logger as a Pro user, your entries are synced to Firebase Firestore (Google Cloud, EU region) to enable cloud backup and cross-device access.
  • Saved locations — favourite flying spots you save are synced to Firebase Firestore.
  • Subscription status — your Pro subscription status (active/cancelled) is stored in Firebase Firestore to control access to Pro features.
  • Payment processing — payments are handled by Stripe. We do not store card details. Stripe stores billing information in accordance with their own privacy policy.

Your Pro account data is stored in Firebase Firestore, hosted in the EU (europe-west2 region, London). You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at bbridgewater.dev@gmail.com. You can cancel your subscription at any time via your Stripe billing portal.

3.5 Device Preferences and Local Data

To improve your experience, the following data is saved locally on your device using browser local storage. None of this data is transmitted to us or any third party:

  • Drone profile — your selected drone model, used to personalise wind limit thresholds.
  • Unit preference — whether you have selected metric (km/h, °C) or imperial (mph, °F) units.
  • Pre-flight checklist state — which checklist items you have ticked, so your progress is preserved between sessions.
  • Disclaimer acceptance — a flag confirming you have read and accepted the advisory disclaimer, so it is not shown on every visit.
  • Onboarding state — a flag confirming you have completed the introductory onboarding screens.
  • Favourite locations — location names and GPS coordinates for any flying spots you have saved. Free users: stored locally only. Pro users: also synced to Firebase Firestore for cross-device access.
  • Flight log entries — if you use the flight logger, your entries (including dates, location names, drone model, flight duration, conditions and any notes you add) are stored locally on your device. Free users: local only, never transmitted to us. Pro users: also synced to Firebase Firestore for cloud backup and cross-device access. You can export or delete entries at any time within the app.
  • Cached weather data — the most recently loaded weather conditions, stored with a timestamp to allow the app to display last known data when offline. This contains weather readings only and no personal information.
  • KP index cache — the most recently fetched geomagnetic KP index value and 3-day forecast from NOAA, stored with a timestamp to allow accurate fly ratings on next load without waiting for a fresh NOAA data fetch. Contains no personal information and expires after 1 hour.

You can clear all locally stored data at any time by clearing your browser or app cache, or by using the "Reset Data" option in the app footer, or by uninstalling the app.

3.6 Cookies

DroneChecker itself does not set any first-party cookies. However, Google Analytics 4 — which is loaded only after you accept the in-app disclaimer — sets the following cookies on your device:

  • _ga — distinguishes unique users. Expires after 2 years.
  • _ga_<container-id> — used to persist session state. Expires after 2 years.

These cookies are analytics cookies only. No advertising, tracking, or profiling cookies are used. You can prevent Google Analytics cookies from being set by using the "Reset Data" option in the app footer (which withdraws your consent and reloads the app), by using your browser's built-in cookie controls, or by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

5. Local Storage, Cloud Storage and Offline Mode

DroneChecker uses your device's local storage in two ways:

  • Service worker cache — app files (HTML, CSS, scripts) are cached locally so the app loads quickly and works offline.
  • User preferences and data — your drone profile, unit preference, checklist state, favourite locations, flight log entries, and the most recent weather data are stored locally to improve your experience and enable offline use (see Section 3.5).

Free users: no personal data is transmitted to DroneChecker's servers. All stored data remains on your device.

Pro subscribers: in addition to local storage, your flight log entries and saved locations are synced to Firebase Firestore (Google Cloud, EU region) to enable cloud backup and cross-device access. Your email address and subscription status are also stored in Firestore. You can request deletion of all cloud data by contacting us at bbridgewater.dev@gmail.com.

6. Children's Privacy

DroneChecker is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at bbridgewater.dev@gmail.com.

7. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights regarding any personal data we hold:

  • Right of access — to request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to erasure — to request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restriction of processing — to request that we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability — to receive your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format and transfer it to another controller where technically feasible.
  • Right to object — to object to processing of your personal data where we rely on legitimate interests as our legal basis.
  • Right to withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent (such as analytics), you may withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing. Use the "Reset Data" option in the app footer to do this.
  • Right to lodge a complaint — with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

Free users: as DroneChecker does not collect or store personal data on our servers for free users, most of these rights are satisfied by the nature of the app itself. Any locally stored preferences and flight log data can be cleared using the "Reset Data" option in the app footer, or by clearing your browser or app cache.

Pro subscribers: you have full rights over the personal data stored in your Pro account (email, flight log, saved locations, subscription status). To exercise any right — including account deletion — please contact us at bbridgewater.dev@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days. You can cancel your subscription at any time via your Stripe billing portal.

For analytics data processed by Google, you may opt out using the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.
